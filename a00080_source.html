10 /*
11  Chains are instances of the
12  model render pipeline
13  You need a different chain for a different size / shape output,
14  or a different thead.
15  When requesting a render of the model,
16  you must use one of its chains.
17 
18  All VideoNode renders happen against a chain.
19  A Chain stores a particular resolution
20  at which the render is to be done.
21  A Chain also stores state that does not belong to any particular VideoNode
22  for the render, i.e. a blank texture and noise texture
23  which is available to all.
24 
25  Chains are immutable once created,
26  that is, you cannot change the size.
27 
28  Chains are created by Outputs or Output-like things
29  (such as the preview adapter.)
30 
31  Chains are thread-safe by nature of being immutable.
32  However, OpenGL thread restrictions still apply
33  (e.g. don't try to access the textures
34  from multiple threads)
35 */
